About Us

The style of cuisine is modern Irish and the restaurant prides itself on using fresh produce in season; most of which is sourced locally. One of the main aims of Lily's is to give quality food and service at a reasonable price. We also offer an outdoor catering service where we deliver to your desired location.

Meet the Team

Chief Proprietor - John Kelly

John trained and worked at the Adare Manor for three years. John was voted the Munster Young Chef of the year in 1997. He then worked abroad for a number of years in a number of 5 star hotels in London and Paris. In March 2007 he returned to ireland and set up Lily's Restaurant

Head Chef - Keith Murphy

Keith first started his career as a chef at The Gibson Hotel, Dublin at the age of 20, he then took up the role as Sous Chef of The Left Side Restaurant and shortly after, he was promoted to Head Chef. In 2008, Keith started in The Adare Manor where he spent 8 years as Sous Chef. Keith joined Lily's as Head Chef in October 2017.

Restaurant Manager - Rebecca Lee

Rebecca first started in The Jury's Hotel, Galway at the age of 17, after two years he went to train and work at The Adare Manor. In May 2018, he returned to Jury's Hotel, Galway as Restaurant Supervisor, she was then promoted to Restaurant Manager in May 2019. Rebecca also takes a keen interest in all Marketing and Social Media aspects of the restaurant
